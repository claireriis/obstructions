About

My name is Claire Riis and I am from Long Island, New York. I am a fan of mixed media projects and incorporating different layers to my graphic design work.

Project Description

The Obstructions Project is a project created by Professor Mitchell Goldstein at Rochester Institute of Technology inspired by the Danish film “The Five Obstructions”. Each week, students are given a new set of obstructions at random that they must incorporate everyday into their work. You can work with the obstructions or around them, but they must be involved in someway. The project as a whole is about idea generation and creating.
